Trinity Fusion (2023)

An ambitious multi-platform multiverse venture.

Fact Sheet

  • Developer: Angry Mob Games
  • Publisher: Angry Mob Games
  • Release date: Q4, 2023
  • Platforms: Steam, PS5, Xbox, Nintendo Switch

Due to the heavy constraints of the mobile devices at the time, AVP Evolution was heavily reliant on trickery to shave off processing costs- lightmapping, occlusion culling and Light Probes were a blessing but also a constant breaking point. The way Unity calculated Occlusion Portals meant that I had to go in each level, test the camera extensively and rebake the Occlusion data and portals nearly daily, paying attention to the doors that might be open or occluding. I was in charge of meticulously checking the flags on all the props to make sure they receive proper lighting and don't pop in and out.

Most visible contribution: Typhoon Two.

cutting room floor ✂

One of the Secrets I originally designed entailed sabotaging a fleet of APCs (Armored Personnel Carriers) as the Predator. Implementing it was time-consuming and it didn't make the cut.

The in-game Predator Razor Whips don't have mesh models, they were made using Unity's Line Renderer component with a generous width and an emissive texture tiled along the line.
