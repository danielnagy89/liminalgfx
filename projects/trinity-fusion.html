Trinity Fusion (2023)

An ambitious multi-platform multiverse venture.

Fact Sheet

  • Developer: Angry Mob Games
  • Publisher: Angry Mob Games
  • Release date: Q4, 2023
  • Platforms: Steam, PS5, Xbox, Nintendo Switch

Easily the most challenging project I've been a part of, Trinity Fusion put my thinking and concepting abilities to the test. From research to co-designing the level editing tool, creating documentation and managing the volume of environment assets, I was deeply involved all the way from idea to market.

Most visible contribution: Typhoon Two, the Overworld boss.

I was in charge of handling this large mechanical fellow from concept art to basemesh, 3d modeling, baking, texturing and base rigging.
Greatly amplified by spiffy animations and energetic VFX, I'm very proud how it turned out and it was one of the most stimulating parts of the project for me.

The Arcstrider

Another design I've made from concept to ingame model- aside from being a more tricky enemy, he's been featured in the trailer.
Obviously influenced by the Half-Life 2 striders, we needed a high-priority target that was a bit harder to reach, buying the lower tier enemies some time to do damage.
