Atrophytwin

Confluence of a couple of firsts - for one, the first and only time I've tried to use the now-defunct Sculptris that later got absorbed into Zbrush. It was rudimentary enough to allow a lumpy mess of a basemesh.

Then I brought the model into Blender, where I made a special SubSurfaceScattering shader for it, and first ever renders. I've composited the renders in Affinity Photo and did some additional painting in Painstorm Studio. It was a neat little experiment.
